# Reflection

One of the main challenges in this assignment was balancing the needs of different stakeholders. For example, users require fast and simple results, while developers require a maintainable and scalable system. Also, ensuring system performance while maintaining high accuracy in fake news detection posed a challenge.

Another challenge was defining requirements that are both specific and measurable. It was important to avoid vague statements and instead provide clear acceptance criteria.

In the end, this process improved understanding of how stakeholder needs influence system design and requirements.
